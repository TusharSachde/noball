    foundation

    All our profits at CCC go towards the betterment of sports infrastructure in rural India. In 2014 we launched our sports initiative to raise funding to build a cricket ground alongside an indoor sports stadium with world standard amenities. Located in Kathiwada, Madhya Pradesh this stadium offers unparalleled beauty and serenity which hopes to capture the imagination of every underprivileged child in the region. Coaching academies and vocational training centers will support the talent that emerges from the area.

    Already in a short span of time Kathiwada has received much attention towards the cause and one such example is Tom Holland, a celebrated author and historian who has founded BATAID along with The Authors Cricket Club of England to generously raise money for the charity annually.

    consultancy

    Our love for the game goes beyond just products: a passion project has been to make cricket more accessible to more people in more countries. We’ve created numerous models for contained “cage cricket” / indoor cricket which as many will know is a world phenomena and even world cups are staged in this format. We’ve worked with a team of architects and consultants to evaluate micro-markets where cage cricket can be a sustainable endeavor for individuals, tenant associations and even municipal governments. Should you want to consider installing a facility such as this in your neighborhood or office block as a business venture or even a private lane for personal use look no further! We can provide a detailed project proposal to make it possible.
