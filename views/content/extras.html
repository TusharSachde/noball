    In-Bound (India)

    Mumbai | Udaipur (Rajasthan) | Kathiwada (Madhya Pradesh).

    Come to incredible India for a fine cricket experience; be it in-stadia to watch the IPL and other international fixtures or even bring your team to play a series spanning three states, we can arrange it all. Custom Cricket Company founders have had a wealth of experience in sports hospitality prior to launching the e-commerce products portal and excel to provide you the full customer experience just the way we did it. Curate your personal tour with us – we’ve done it and we’re just a phone call away!

    Play matches at famous city maidaans of Mumbai, the capital cricket city of India and enjoy full treats the metro has to offer with our friendly ground team to show you around. Visit principalities and stay in palaces of Udaipur and Kathiwada for a full royal experience where you can indulge in rural life to capture the true essence of India with fixtures at their most exquisite cricket grounds.
